Unordered Lists

These lists are used for items that do not have a particular oder.
Types of Unordered Lists:
  • Disc
  • Circle
  • square
  • none

    • Ordered Lists

    Types of ordered lists:

              Lowercase Numerals

    Nested Lists

    We can place lists inside another list to create a sublist

    1. First Period: 8:20-9:48
    2. Second Period: 9:55-11:27
    3. Block Lunch:
      1. A Block: 11:27-11:57
      2. B Block: 11:57-12:27
    4. Third Period: 12:32-2:00
    5. Fourth Period: 2:07-3:35
